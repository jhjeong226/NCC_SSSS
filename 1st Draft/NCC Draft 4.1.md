# Study Area

The study area covers 610 hectares of rice paddy in Seosan coastal reclaimed land (36°64′ N, 126°47’ E), which was constructed in 1995 for agricultural purposes. Seosan is located in the central part of the western coast of the Republic of Korea and exhibits the characteristics of a marine climate. The annual average temperature is 12.1°C. The highest monthly average temperature is 25.3°C in August, while the coldest month, January, records -1.6°C. The annual average precipitation is 1,253.7 mm, with summer rainfall accounting for 56% of the total annual precipitation. The Yellow Sea, which flows near Seosan, has short-term seasonal variations in seawater conditions due to monsoon-driven winds, heat exchange with the atmosphere, and precipitation. Despite Seosan reclaimed tideland being constructed 30 years ago, it still suffers from salinity issues.
