Figure 1. Recent relationships between precipitation, salinity, and rice yield (2018-2023). (a) Heatmap showing correlation coefficients between monthly precipitation and rice yield for Golden Queen and Dongjinchal cultivars. Significant correlations are highlighted in yellow (p < 0.05*, p < 0.01**, p < 0.001***), with strong positive correlations in April (r = 0.84, p < 0.001) transitioning to negative correlations in September (r = -0.71, p < 0.01). (b) Heatmap showing correlation coefficients between monthly reservoir salinity and rice yield, with significant negative correlation in May (r = -0.70, p < 0.05) and positive correlation in August (r = 0.69, p < 0.05). (c) Seasonal variation of reservoir salinity (red line) and 5-month cumulative precipitation (blue bars), demonstrating inverse relationship throughout the year. (d) Scatter plot showing strong negative correlation (r = 0.98) between reservoir salinity and 5-month cumulative precipitation with monthly data points labeled.
