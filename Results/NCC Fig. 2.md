Figure 2. Long-term trends in precipitation, salinity, and rice yield relationships (1997-2023). (a) Heatmap showing predominantly negative correlations between precipitation and rice yield over the long term, with significant negative correlations in August-September. (b) Heatmap showing positive correlations between reservoir salinity and rice yield, with strongest positive correlations in August-September. (c) Long-term seasonal patterns of reservoir salinity (red line) and 5-month cumulative precipitation (blue bars), showing consistent inverse relationship. (d) Scatter plot of salinity versus 5-month cumulative precipitation showing strong negative correlation (r = 0.80) with monthly data points labeled.
