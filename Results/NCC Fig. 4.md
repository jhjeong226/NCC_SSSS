Figure 4. Environmental changes between the first decade (1997-2006, light gray) and last decade (2014-2023, dark gray) of the study period. Bar graphs show mean values with standard deviation for key environmental variables. Statistically significant increases were observed in salinity (p < 0.001) and sea level (p < 0.01), while precipitation, air temperature (Ta), and vapor pressure deficit (VPD) showed no statistically significant changes. This suggests sea-level rise as a primary driver of increased salinity stress in coastal rice cultivation systems, rather than changes in precipitation patterns or other climatic variables.
